---
title: Types Of Tilt
---
- **Injustice Tilt:** Acknowledge statistical variance rather than adopting a victim mentality regarding standard probabilistic distributions.
    
- **Hate-Losing Tilt:** Accept the mathematical inevitability of negative short-term outcomes within an optimal strategic framework.
    
- **Mistake Tilt:** Defer critical performance analysis and error correction until the completion of the active session.
    
- **Entitlement Tilt:** Recognize that mathematically inferior opponent decisions yield a positive long-term expected value, regardless of short-term results.
    
- **Revenge Tilt:** Maintain baseline strategic parameters and explicitly avoid retaliatory deviations targeting specific adversaries.
    
- **Desperation Tilt:** Mandate an immediate operational cessation to mitigate further compound negative variance.
    
- **Fearful Tilt:** Suspend active play to recalibrate risk tolerance and restore a mathematically objective decision-making paradigm.
    
- **Invincible Tilt:** Discard the gambler's fallacy and recognize that statistical variance operates independently of recent positive outcomes.
