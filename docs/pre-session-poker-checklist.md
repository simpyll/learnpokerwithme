# Pre-Session Poker Checklist

## Remember!

You're playing this game to #1 learn and #2 optimize decision making. You're not here to make money. 

### Mental & Physical Readiness
 * ✅ **Physical Check:** Rested, hydrated, and adequately nourished prior to initiating the session.
 * ✅ **Mental Check:** Cleared of tilt from previous sessions and free of external life stressors.
 * ✅ **Warm-up:** Completed a brief 15-minute physical warm-up to ensure optimal cognitive alertness.
### Bankroll & Progression Limits
 * ✅ **The 2NL Lock:** Strictly playing 2NL 6-Max No Limit Hold'em. Will not ascend stakes until PokerTracker 4 logs 10,000 hands completed. No exceptions for confidence or positive variance.
 * ✅ **Stop-Loss Protocol:** Strict stop-loss defined and enacted at ___ buy-ins.
 * ✅ **Session Constraints:** Defined maximum of ___ tables for a strict duration of ___ minutes.
### Process Goals
 * ✅ **Actionable Goal:** Define one specific "will do" task for the session (e.g., categorize every opponent at the table within the first 20 hands).
 * ✅ **Avoidance Goal:** Define one specific "won't do" restriction (e.g., execute zero turn or river bets without articulating a clear value or bluff rationale).
 * ✅ **Mindset Reset:** Focus entirely on executing profitable Game Theory Optimal (GTO) decisions based on available data. Monetary outcomes are irrelevant to session success.
### Game Selection & Tilt Management
 * ✅ **Table Selection:** Actively identify soft tables; immediately depart tough, unprofitable, or overly aggressive games.
 * ✅ **Tilt Trigger:** Pre-defined action plan established (e.g., if feeling frustrated by negative variance, immediately sit out for 10 minutes or close one table).
### Post-Session Closeout
 * ✅ **Hand Review:** Tag 3 to 5 specific hands during play for subsequent off-table analysis.
 * ✅ **Performance Audit:** Document one identified mistake pattern and one successfully executed strategy immediately upon concluding the session.
