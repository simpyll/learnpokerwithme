## Notes and Observations to Optimize 2 NL play


- JJ is too weak to go all in preflop with 100bb effective stacks
- Its okay to call 3b and 4b pots AK instead of getting it in preflop against a reg to reduce variance 
- Play TAG ABC poker.
- Play tight.
- Don't limp.
- Bluff less.
- Value bet more (bet/raise when you have something).
- Be aggressive. 
- Don't hero call. 
- Don't do fancy plays.
