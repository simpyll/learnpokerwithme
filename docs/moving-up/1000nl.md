* **Dynamic Sizing Algorithms:** Preflop isolation and squeeze mechanics utilize dynamic sizing algorithms calculated to the exact big blind fraction, calibrated to deny precise equity margins based on the specific positional configuration of the opponents.

**3. Postflop Deep-Tree Heuristics and Computational Strategy Execution**

* **Zero-Margin Indifference:** River defense parameters are calibrated strictly to absolute Minimum Defense Frequencies (MDF). Strategy relies on pushing the opponent's entire bluff-catching matrix to exactly zero EV.
* **Asymmetrical Node-Locking:** Deviations from equilibrium are mathematically dangerous. Exploitative adjustments are only deployed via computational node-locking when proprietary database samples confirming hundreds of thousands of hands prove a specific operator's micro-frequential failure to defend MDF at isolated nodes.
